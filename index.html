Pendency c

IRI http://example.com/certi/commissioning#Pendency
Description

An identified issue, defect, or non-conformity that arises during inspections, testing, or commissioning. It represents a task or action that needs to be addressed before the project can be considered complete. Pendency items are typically documented, tracked, and resolved to ensure compliance with design specifications, safety standards, and operational requirements. They are essential for quality assurance and help to guarantee that any outstanding matters are rectified before final handover.
